# 📊 Retail Sales Analysis – SQL Case Study

## 📌 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data using SQL to derive actionable insights related to product performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends. The analysis helps in making data-driven decisions for improving sales and customer engagement.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Identify high and low performing products
- Analyze customer purchase behavior
- Segment customers based on buying patterns
- Understand sales trends and growth patterns

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning

The following data preprocessing steps were performed:

- Removed duplicate transactions
- Fixed inconsistent product pricing across tables
- Handled missing/null values (replaced with 'Unknown')
- Standardized date formats for analysis

---

## 📊 Analysis Performed

- Product-wise revenue and quantity analysis
- Customer purchase frequency analysis
- Category-wise performance evaluation
- Top 10 and least selling products
- Daily sales trend analysis
- Month-over-Month (MoM) growth calculation
- Customer segmentation (Low, Medium, High value)
- Repeat purchase and customer loyalty analysis

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Identified top-performing products contributing maximum revenue
- Detected low-performing products needing marketing focus
- Segmented customers into Low, Medium, and High-value groups
- Observed variations in customer purchase frequency
- Analyzed revenue trends and growth patterns over time
